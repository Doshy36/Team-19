Tagline 1: Go Discover!

Blurb 1: Discover for yourself the wonderful place that is Northumberland, with our simple and handy app. Discover Northumberland is designed to help you get most out of your visit. Easing the process of finding anything from famous landmarks, to a nice place to have lunch.

Features:

Navigation:
Explore Northumberland with helpful directions to anywhere that takes your fancy, with a free roam map to search around the county and local area you are in.

Filter:
Choose a theme of locations to visit, whether it be historical, sporting, cultural or cuisine.

Learning:
Listen and read about the history of each attraction with our personal audio tour as it explains the location you are at.

Bookmark:
Save locations as a favourite, or a place you would like to visit again. Our app will remember it for you!

Disability Access:
If you need help planning around a difficulty you have, check out our disibility filter as we plan ahead for you!

Dark Mode (not sure if we need this): 
Choose to rest your eyes when using in the dark with our carefully designed dark mode!
