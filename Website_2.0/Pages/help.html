Map


How to zoom in and out on the map

To zoom in:
Within the displayed map area
1. Place two fingers next to each other on the screen
2. Drag your fingers apart

To zoom out:
Within the displayed map area
1. Place two fingers on the screen. Leave a little distance between your fingers.
2. Drag your fingers together

How can I tell if there is a place nearby

On the displayed map, a red pin will be visible if there a location near you.

Sometimes you might not be directly next to a location. If you move the map or zoom out a new area will be visible and you could now possibly see locations

There are no places visible

This could be for a couple of reasons:

1. Your devices GPS is not turned on.
You will need to go to your devices location settings and turn it on

2. You haven't allowed the app to know your location.
You will need to go into your devices settings, head to your location settings and allow this app to know your location

3. You are not in Northumberland
The app only displays locations within Northumberland, Newcastle Upon Tyne, and Tyne and Wear. If you are not in one of those areas, the map will automatically center at your location. To see the locations, you will need to zoom out a lot and move the map until Northumberland is visible

How to see the location name at a pin

Tap the pin and the name of the location it is pointing to will be displayed above it

Tap the name of the location to see more details about said place

How to see see more details about a location

Using the map:
1. Tap a pin to display its name
2. Tap the name label and more details about the location will be displayed, such as an image and a description

Another way:
1. Choose a category (Heritage, Culture, Cusine, Sport)
2. Scroll and find a place you want to know about
3. Tap it. This will display more infomation about said locations, such as an image and a description

Accessability


How to make the text bigger

At the moment making the text larger isn't something you can do. Instead we have implemented a text to speech feature.

How to listen to the location descriptions

1. Tap the listen icon . It is located at the right hand side of the screen, directly below the locations title

How to stop the text being read aloud

1. Tap the listen icon . It is located at the right hand side of the screen, directly below the locations title

Note: It will say "stop" below the icon (rather than "listen")
