Discover for yourself the wonderful county that is Northumberland, with our simple and handy app.
The app is designed to help you get most out of your visit by easing the process of finding locations of interest close to you!
From well known historical landmarks to niche cultural locations, the app has places for everyone

FAQ

Will the app run on IOS or other devices?

No. Unfortuently at the moment the app will only run on devices that use Android operating systems. Specifically Andriod ver.7 upwards.

What versions of Android does the app work on?

It will work on version 7 upwards. This includes:
- Android Nougat (ver 7.0)
- Android Nougat (ver 7.1)
- Android Oreo (ver 8.0)
- Android Oreo (ver 8.1)
- Android Pie (ver 9.0)
- Android 10 (ver 10.0)
- Android 11 (ver 11.0)

What area does the app cover?

The app covers locations within the county of Northumberland, Newcastle Upon Tyne, and also ventures into the Tyne and Wear area.

What kind of locations are in the app?

The app seperates locations by 4 categories:
- Historical
- Cultural
- Sports
- Cuisine
These locations range from castles to harbours, statues to stadiums, restaurants to filming locations, and so on. There is something for everyone.

Do you need to create an account/log in to use the app?

No. You can view infomation about locations around you without having to create an account/log in. However, if you wish to rate/review a location or bookmark it, you are required to log in.

Does the app work without mobile data/wifi?

No

Why does the app need to know my location?

A main feature of the the app is that it displays locations that are near to you (within Northumberland). Therefore for the app to be able to show recommended places and details such as travelling distance, the app needs to know where you currently are. It uses your devices built in GPS. Your location is not shared with anyone.
