Account


How to create an account

1. Tap the menu icon. This will bring up the side menu on the left of the screen
2. Click ‘Login In’
3. Enter your email and a password
4. Press ‘Register’
5. Press ‘Ok’ to continue to use the app

Note: You will be automatically logged in with that account after pressing register. Make sure you remember your password

How to log in

1. Tap the menu icon. This will bring up the side menu on the left of the screen
2. Click ‘Login In’
3. Enter your email and a password
4. Press ‘Log In’
5. Press ‘Ok’ to continue to use the app

How to log out

1. Tap the menu icon. This will bring up the side menu on the left of the screen
2. Click ‘Log out’, another screen will appear telling you that you are currently logged in. This screen is there to get your approval before logging out in the case of accidentally pressing “log out”.
3. Press ‘Log Out’

Map


How to zoom in and out on the map

To zoom in:
Within the displayed map area
1. Place two fingers next to each other on the screen
2. Drag your fingers apart

To zoom out:
Within the displayed map area
1. Place two fingers on the screen. Leave a little distance between your fingers.
2. Drag your fingers together

How can I tell if there is a place nearby

On the displayed map, a red pin will be visible if there a location near you.

Sometimes you might not be directly next to a location. If you move the map or zoom out a new area will be visible and you could now possibly see locations

There are no places visible

This could be for a couple of reasons:

1. Your devices GPS is not turned on.
You will need to go to your devices location settings and turn it on

2. You haven't allowed the app to know your location.
You will need to go into your devices settings, head to your location settings and allow this app to know your location

3. You are not in Northumberland
The app only displays locations within Northumberland, Newcastle Upon Tyne, and Tyne and Wear. If you are not in one of those areas, the map will automatically center at your location. To see the locations, you will need to zoom out a lot and move the map until Northumberland is visible

How to see the location name at a pin

Tap the pin and the name of the location it is pointing to will be displayed above it

Tap the name of the location to see more details about said place

How to see see more details about a location

Using the map:
1. Tap a pin to display its name
2. Tap the name label and more details about the location will be displayed, such as an image and a description

Another way:
1. Choose a category (Heritage, Culture, Cuisine, Sport)
2. Scroll and find a place you want to know about
3. Tap it. This will display more information about said locations, such as an image and a description

Locations


How to look for a specific place

Using the category filters:
1. Choose a category (Heritage, Culture, Cuisine, Sport)
2. Scroll and find the place you want to know about
3. Tap it. This will display more information about said locations, such as an image and a description
Note: Some places will fit into more than one category

Using the map:
If you know the location of the place you are looking for, you can find it's pin on the map.
1. Tap a pin to display its name
2. Tap the name label and more details about the location will be displayed, such as an image and a description

Ratings


How to rate a location

You need to be logged in to do this

1. Open the location you want to rate
2. Tap on the “rate” icon
3. Choose how many stars you want to give the place by tapping the star icons
4. Tap ‘submit’

How to see other peoples ratings

Once you have opened a location you can see the average of all ratings left on that location above the title. It is displayed in a 5 star system with the decimal rating to the left of it

Note: You cannot see each individual ratings left by other users. Only the average of all is displayed

How to change a rating/make another rating

You can’t. So when you rate a location be sure to be honest

How to remove a rating

You can’t. So when you rate a location be sure to be honest

Bookmarks


How to add a bookmark

You need to be logged in to do this

1. Open the location you want to bookmark
2. Tap on the “bookmark” icon this will automatically add it to your bookmark list

How to remove a bookmark

You need to be logged in to do this

1. Open the location you want to bookmark
2. Tap on the “bookmark” icon this will bring up a box that will ask if you want to delete it
3. Tap 'Delete'

How to view my bookmarks

You need to be logged in to do this

1. Tap the menu icon. This will bring up the side menu on the left of the screen
2. Click bookmarks to view yours

How to stop the text being read aloud

1. Tap the listen icon . It is located at the right hand side of the screen, directly below the locations title

Note: It will say "stop" below the icon (rather than "listen")
